Professional New Roof Installation

Galveston's Standard in Professional Roof Installation

Whether you are building a new residential home, commercial structure, or adding an accessory building, FJ Roofing is the premier choice for new roof installations in Galveston, TX. We bring years of coastal construction experience to every project.

Installing a roof in a coastal environment requires specific materials and execution techniques that inland contractors often overlook. Owners Avian Figueroa and Denali Figueroa supervise our crews to ensure that every installation adheres to strict engineering guidelines, including heavy-duty underlayments and salt-resistant stainless steel fasteners.

Our Structural Installation Standards

A quality roof is a complete system. We don't just lay down shingles; we build a layered defense system for your home:

  • Decking Reinforcement: We verify the roof deck thickness and renail the decking to structural rafters using hot-dipped galvanized ring-shank nails to prevent uplift.
  • Premium Underlayment: We install a synthetic tear-resistant underlayment, plus self-adhering ice & water shield membranes in valleys, eaves, and penetrations.
  • Starter Strip Integration: We secure heavy-duty starter shingles along all edges to resist wind uplift.
  • Advanced Ridge Ventilation: We cut and install continuous ridge ventilation systems to allow hot air to escape, reducing humidity and lowering your cooling bills.

Our New Roof Installation Process

From initial inspection to TWIA compliance submission, we execute every step with complete transparency and compliance. Here is what to expect when working with FJ Roofing:

1
Free Estimate & Site Inspection

We assess the building, review structural layouts, and provide a clear, comprehensive quote outlining materials, timeline, and pricing.

2
Engineering Review

3
Material Preparation

We deliver premium, certified materials to the site, taking care to protect your landscaping, driveway, and structural surroundings.

Expert Installation

Our experienced crew installs the underlayment, starter shingles, roofing panels or shingles, flashing, and ridge ventilation under direct supervision.

5
Quality Audit & Cleanup

We perform a multi-point inspection, run magnetic rollers to collect all nails, and clear all debris from your property.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our services or windstorm compliance? Read our answers below or give owners Avian & Denali a call direct.

What roofing materials are best for Galveston's climate?

For residential properties, heavy-duty architectural asphalt shingles (with wind ratings up to 130 MPH) or standing seam metal roofs are best. For commercial properties, multi-ply modified bitumen or TPO single-ply systems are popular and highly durable.

How long does a standard residential roof installation take?

Most residential roof installations are completed in 1 to 3 days, depending on the size of the home and weather conditions.

Do you secure the necessary city permits?

Yes, we handle all permitting with the City of Galveston and register the project with the Texas Department of Insurance (TDI) for windstorm inspections.
