The FJ Roofing Story

Built on Family, Local Pride, and Structural Integrity.

FJ Roofing was founded on a simple principle: coastal homes need roofers who understand coastal weather. Owners Avian Figueroa and Denali Figueroa grew up in Galveston, experiencing firsthand the power of Gulf Coast storms. Together, they built FJ Roofing to deliver a higher standard of protection.

We do not cut corners. We are not storm chasers who disappear after the rain stops. We live here, we work here, and we guarantee every nail, seam, and shingle we install. As licensed, insured, and certified windstorm specialists, we stand behind the durability of our coastal builds.

Don't Fight Your Insurance Alone

Storm damage claims along the Texas Gulf Coast can be incredibly complex. Between TWIA requirements and private carrier policies, property owners face heavy stress.

FJ Roofing specializes in negotiating with adjusters and documenting storm damage. We act as your advocate, providing detailed drone reports, thermal imaging, and physical code documentation to ensure your roof is fully repaired or replaced with zero coverage gaps.

  • Pre-inspection damage reports and photos
  • Direct adjusters meeting & coordination
  • TWIA compliance & engineering certificates
